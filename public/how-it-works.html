How the
Process Works

When you choose Antifra, a dedicated case manager will guide you through the entire process, ensuring clarity and support at every step. Our streamlined approach maximizes the chances of recovering your lost assets effectively.

Free Consultation

Start your claim, and our specialist will contact you for an initial assessment of your situation.

Data Collection

We sign an official legal services contract and start gathering evidence of broker misconduct.

Recovery Procedure

We identify the recipient of the funds and initiate the recovery process.

Free Consultation

Your journey begins with a free consultation, where our specialist evaluates your case and provides a clear outline of the process. You'll be assigned a personal case manager who will serve as your main point of contact, keeping you informed and assisting with every stage of your claim.

Data Collection

We gather all relevant information and documentation from you to build a strong case. Our legal team becomes involved immediately, ensuring every detail is addressed. During this phase, we help you navigate any bureaucratic hurdles, whether with government agencies, banks, or other institutions, to ensure the necessary groundwork is in place.

Investigation

Our team conducts a thorough investigation to identify the ultimate recipient of your funds. Using advanced tools and legal expertise, we trace the money trail and gather evidence to support your claim. This phase often involves uncovering hidden trails and building a case that cannot be ignored.

Recovery Procedure

Once the investigation is complete, we prepare all necessary documents and initiate the recovery process. Leveraging legal channels, regulatory authorities, and established protocols, we work to ensure your funds are retrieved efficiently and securely.

Results

Our efforts often lead to brokers voluntarily returning not only the original deposit but, in many cases, a portion of any investment income as well. This outcome is driven by the strength of the case and the potential public scrutiny it could attract. Once the recovery is complete, you will pay the lawyer's fee from the successfully recovered funds, ensuring fairness and transparency throughout the process.
