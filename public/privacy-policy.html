Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

At AntiFra ("we," "us," or "our"), your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our services. We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), among other applicable regulations.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name

  • Email address

  • Phone number

1.2 Non-Personal Information

  • IP address

  • Browser type and version

  • Device information

  • Pages visited and time spent

1.3 Special Categories of Information

  • Transaction details, wallet addresses, and reports related to cryptocurrency fraud.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our services.

  • Analyze usage trends to enhance user experience.

  • Develop new products and services based on user needs.

  • Comply with legal obligations and assist in fraud investigations.

  • Communicate with you and respond to inquiries.

  • Improve security and prevent fraudulent activities.

  • Conduct marketing, research, and promotional campaigns, subject to your consent.

  • Sell or share your information, as allowed under applicable regulations.

  • Limited to non-sensitive data such as name, email, and phone number, with consent or as permitted by law.

3. Sharing and Selling Your Information

We may share and sell your information in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers who assist in delivering our services, under strict confidentiality agreements.

  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce agreements.

  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of AntiFra, its users, or others.

  • With affiliates and business partners to improve service offerings, subject to applicable laws.

  • As part of a business transaction, such as a merger or acquisition, with appropriate safeguards.

  • With any entity, organization, or individual, as permissible under applicable laws and regulations.

4. Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) under GDPR, to protect your information.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

  • Access your data and obtain a copy.

  • Request corrections to your information.

  • Request deletion of your personal data (right to be forgotten).

  • Restrict processing of your data.

  • Opt-out of the sale or sharing of your personal information.

  • Withdraw consent for data processing, where applicable.

  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant authority.

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@antifra-consult.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. In some cases, we may anonymize data for analytics and research purposes.

7. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data, including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and tracking technologies to enhance functionality, provide a personalized experience, and analyze website usage. These may include:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website operation.

  • Performance Cookies: For analytics and usage trends.

  • Marketing Cookies: To deliver targeted advertising and facilitate information sharing and sales.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie management tool.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with information, contact us to have it removed.

10. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in laws or services. Changes will be effective upon posting with a revised "Effective Date." We encourage regular review to stay informed about how we protect your information.

By using our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. For questions or concerns, contact us at privacy@antifra-consult.com.
