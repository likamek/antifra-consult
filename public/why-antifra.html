Why to Use AntiFra?

At Antifra, we understand the huge impact that losing money to fraud can have on victims.

It’s important that you are supported by an ethical company that charges fair fees, operates transparently, and supports you throughout the process.

Expertise and Experience

Our team brings decades of combined experience in law, financial recovery, fraud investigation, and blockchain technologies, ensuring your case is handled with the utmost professionalism.

Transparency and Fairness

We believe in complete transparency. You’ll always know the status of your case, and we clearly outline all costs upfront. With our no-recovery, no-fee policy, you only pay if we successfully recover your funds.

Proven Results

We’ve helped over 4,000 clients recover their lost assets, with a 96% success rate in resolved cases. Our methods have consistently delivered results for victims of complex scams worldwide.

Personalized Support

Each case is unique, and we provide tailored solutions to meet your specific needs. You’ll work directly with experts who are committed to supporting you every step of the way.

Global Reach, Local Care

With clients in over 20 countries, our global expertise ensures we can handle cases regardless of where you are or where the scam originated.

Confidentiality Guaranteed
